About Us

Established in 2017 and officially incorporated in 2023, MAB Polytech specializes in manufacturing high-quality plastic compounding granules. Our products are known for exceptional quality and versatility, suitable for any mold or application.

Our granules feature outstanding physical and chemical properties that meet the demanding requirements of both industrial and consumer applications. We take pride in our skilled team's commitment to service excellence and customer satisfaction.

We continuously enhance our product quality and customize our offerings to meet your unique requirements, ensuring you receive superior service and products that exceed expectations.
